Lund, Karl Gustaf, chief engineer, living in Varberg, born on July 22, 1893, in Hille, Gävle County, Sweden, son of clerk Ferdinand L. and Maria Andersson. Married in 1936 to Sigrid Johansson. Children: Ingvar (born 1938), Lennart (born 1942). — Graduated from Bergsskolan in Filipstad in 1917, specialized studies at the Royal Institute of Technology (KTH) from 1920 to 1922, studied at the Institute of Metallurgy and Stockholm University in 1921-1922.
